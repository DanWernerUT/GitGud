# Questions to Ask When Showing Prototype #

* Do you like the color scheme?
* What is the first thing that catches your eye?
* Would you use this app daily, weekly, or never?
* What do you like/dislike about the app?
* What reward would you want for completing your goals?
* Do the icons make sense?
* Do you have need for an app like this?
* What was the most natural part of the app experience?
* What was the most awkward part of the app experience?
